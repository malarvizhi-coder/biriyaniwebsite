About Us

At DC Biriyani, we bring the essence of traditional biriyani to your plate. Our culinary journey started with a passion for delivering the finest biriyani that tantalizes your taste buds with every bite. Using secret family recipes and the freshest ingredients, we craft each dish with love and care.

A timeless favorite, our Chicken Biriyani is a harmonious blend of aromatic spices, tender chicken, and fragrant basmati rice. Mutton Biriyani: Indulge in the rich and hearty flavors of our Mutton Biriyani, where succulent pieces of mutton are cooked to perfection with a symphony of spices.
